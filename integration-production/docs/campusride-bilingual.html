Abstract摘要

Small-town university campuses present a distinctive coordination challenge: commercial rideshare services are perceived as expensive and intermittently unavailable, yet students—especially international students—already coordinate rides, trades, and activities through informal messaging groups. This paper makes three contributions. First, we present CampusRide, an identity-verified multi-module campus platform covering carpool, marketplace, activities, groups, messaging, and a cross-module points system, all built on a shared .edu identity layer. Second, grounded in a formative survey (N=111 eligible; 79% Mandarin-native among those reporting native language), we report a deep-dive case on the carpool module. The survey reveals that all seven safety-related willingness-to-pay items exceed 50 on a 0–100 scale, that financial motivation dominates other incentive types, and—most distinctively—that a Driver/Both subset (N=19) exhibits the lowest tolerance for unfair ratings (mean = 29.1) compared with late arrivals (47.2), destination changes (41.4), or non-standard routes (52.3), resonating with algorithmic-management concerns documented for professional gig workers. Third, we contribute research-agent, an eight-phase thesis-conditioned evidence pipeline that produced an auditable literature base for this paper, including systematic contradiction surfacing for adversarial scoping. We discuss limitations including the absence of deployment evaluation, three-layer sample skew, and the risk that formalizing grassroots practices may reproduce platform-mediated harms.

小城镇大学校园呈现出一种独特的协调挑战：商业网约车服务被认为昂贵且时常不可用，而学生——尤其是国际学生——早已通过非正式消息群协调拼车、二手交易与活动。本文做出三项贡献。第一，我们提出 CampusRide：一个经身份验证的多模块校园平台，覆盖拼车、二手市场、活动、群组、消息以及跨模块积分系统，全部构建于共享的 .edu 身份层之上。第二，基于一项形成性调查（formative survey；N=111 名合格受访者；在报告母语的受访者中 79% 以普通话为母语），我们报告了对拼车模块的深入案例研究。调查显示：全部七项与安全相关的支付意愿（willingness-to-pay）条目在 0–100 量表上均超过 50 分；经济动机压过其他各类激励；最具特色的是，「司机／两者皆是」（Driver/Both）子样本（N=19）对不公平评分的容忍度最低（均值 29.1），低于乘客迟到（47.2）、目的地变更（41.4）或非常规路线（52.3），这与文献中针对职业零工劳动者记录的算法管理（algorithmic management）担忧相呼应。第三，我们贡献 research-agent：一个八阶段、以论题为条件（thesis-conditioned）的证据流水线，为本文产出了可审计的文献基础，包括服务于对抗性界定（adversarial scoping）的系统性矛盾揭示。我们讨论的局限包括：缺乏部署评估、三层样本偏斜，以及将草根实践形式化可能复制平台中介伤害的风险。

1Introduction引言

Ithaca, New York—home to Cornell University—is a small city where commercial rideshare availability is neither guaranteed nor affordable for students who commute within the city or travel to distant metropolitan areas during breaks. This framing extends beyond transportation: international students in such settings coordinate rides, buy and sell used goods, organize social activities, and seek information through a single social channel, typically a WeChat or WhatsApp group [Liu2023, Bonini2023]. The resulting campus coordination gap is multi-faceted: it is not solved by a single-function app but rather calls for an integrated coordination infrastructure scoped to the campus community.

纽约州伊萨卡（Ithaca）——康奈尔大学（Cornell University）所在地——是一座小城市：对于在市内通勤、或在假期前往远方大都市的学生而言，商业网约车（rideshare）既不保证可用，也并不便宜。这一框架超出了交通范畴：处于这类环境中的国际学生，往往通过单一社交渠道（通常是微信或 WhatsApp 群）来协调拼车、买卖二手物品、组织社交活动并获取信息 [Liu2023, Bonini2023]。由此产生的「校园协调缺口」（campus coordination gap）是多方面的：它无法由单一功能的应用解决，而是需要一套面向校园社区范围的整合协调基础设施。

We address that gap from three angles: a formative survey of students in the Cornell/Ithaca area (N=111 eligible respondents; 79% Mandarin-native among the 91 who reported native language) probing transportation pain points, willingness-to-pay for safety and trust features, motivational structure, and driver-side tolerance for passenger-generated friction; the design and implementation of CampusRide, a six-module campus platform unified by a shared .edu identity layer and four common design primitives; and research-agent, an eight-phase evidence pipeline supporting this paper's literature synthesis.

本文从三个角度回应这一缺口：一项面向康奈尔／伊萨卡地区学生的形成性调查（N=111 名合格受访者；在报告母语的 91 人中 79% 以普通话为母语），用以理解交通痛点、对安全与信任功能的支付意愿、动机结构，以及司机侧对乘客引发摩擦的容忍度；据此设计并实现、由共享 .edu 身份层与四项共通设计基元（design primitives）统一起来的六模块校园平台 CampusRide；以及支撑本文文献综合的八阶段证据流水线 research-agent。

We claim three contributions:

  1. A design case of an identity-verified six-module campus platform, demonstrating how a single .edu identity layer and shared design primitives can structure coordination infrastructure across campus-relevant domains.
  2. A formative, survey-grounded deep-dive into the carpool module, surfacing three transferable design observations: .edu verification as a trust primitive, grassroots coordination practices among international students, and a counterintuitive sensitivity of occasional campus drivers (N=19) toward rating fairness.
  3. research-agent, a thesis-conditioned eight-phase evidence pipeline that provides an auditable literature synthesis workflow, contributed as a reusable methodology tool for the design-research community.

我们主张三项贡献：

  1. 一个经身份验证的六模块校园平台设计案例，展示单一 .edu 身份层与共享设计基元如何在校园相关领域之间组织协调基础设施。
  2. 一项基于调查的、形成性的拼车模块深入研究，提炼出三项可迁移的设计观察：作为信任基元（trust primitive）的 .edu 验证、国际学生中的草根协调实践，以及偶发性校园司机（N=19）对评分公平性表现出的反直觉敏感性。
  3. research-agent：一个以论题为条件的八阶段证据流水线，提供可审计的文献综合工作流，作为可复用的方法学工具贡献给设计研究社区。

2Related Work相关工作

2.1The Campus Coordination Gap and the Case for an Integrated Platform校园协调缺口与整合平台的必要性

Step 1 — A coordination gap exists. A transportation coordination gap exists across university and small-city settings: mobility needs are local, recurrent, and socially embedded, yet available services are fragmented across campus shuttles, sparse public transit, and commercial rideshare [Khan2023, ChagnonLessard2021]. Campus-transit satisfaction studies document that service quality and schedule alignment vary substantially across institutions, pointing to structural friction rather than incidental inconvenience [Khan2023]. In sparsely populated areas, shared first- and last-mile systems are treated as service-design challenges rather than solved markets [Pecherski2025], and meta-analytic work on Mobility-as-a-Service deployments suggests that integrated mobility systems can improve accessibility but that the evidence is not campus-specific [Witlox2024]. This framing resonates with our formative survey, where Uber was often described as expensive (28 of 32 respondents selecting “Slightly higher” or “Much higher”) and sometimes unavailable or unreliable (23 of 32 reporting at least moderate difficulty), though we return to that evidence in §4.1.

第一步——缺口确实存在。大学与小城市环境普遍存在交通协调缺口：出行需求是本地化、反复发生且嵌入社会关系的，而可用服务却分散在校园班车、稀疏的公共交通与商业网约车之间 [Khan2023, ChagnonLessard2021]。校园交通满意度研究表明，服务质量与时刻表匹配度在不同院校之间差异显著，这指向结构性摩擦而非偶发不便 [Khan2023]。在人口稀疏地区，共享的「首末公里」（first- and last-mile）系统被视为服务设计挑战而非已被解决的市场 [Pecherski2025]；关于「出行即服务」（Mobility-as-a-Service, MaaS）部署的元分析（meta-analysis）研究表明，整合出行系统可以改善可达性，但相关证据并非针对校园情境 [Witlox2024]。这一框架与我们的形成性调查相互呼应：Uber 常被描述为昂贵（32 名受访者中 28 人选择「略高」或「高得多」），且有时不可用或不可靠（32 人中 23 人报告至少中等程度的困难）——我们将在 §4.1 回到这些证据。

Crucially, this gap is not confined to transportation: students in small-town campuses face coordination friction in secondhand trading, event organization, and everyday social coordination as well, which together form a broader campus coordination gap. It is the multi-faceted nature of this gap—not transportation alone—that sets up the second step of the argument.

关键在于，这一缺口并不局限于交通：小城镇校园中的学生在二手交易、活动组织与日常社交协调中同样面临协调摩擦，这些共同构成了更广义的「校园协调缺口」（campus coordination gap）。正是这一缺口的多面性——而非交通本身——引出了论证的第二步。

Step 2 — The gap calls for an integrated platform. Studies of overseas students and adjacent mobile-messaging contexts document that everyday platforms such as WeChat and WhatsApp already carry practical coordination, risk management, and help-seeking across borders [Liu2023, Shi2018, Jin2021]. Chinese overseas students assemble “mobility repertoires” under constraint, deploying distributed social ties for information, emotional support, and logistical coordination [Liu2023]. Mobile instant messaging is documented as boundary-crossing infrastructure for informal problem-solving and knowledge exchange [MobileIM2018], and private messaging groups expand into mutual aid, resistance, and community-of-practice functions when formal platforms leave coordination gaps [Bonini2023]. In our formative survey, 17 of 72 Mandarin-native respondents reported using WeChat or WhatsApp groups to find carpool partners, compared with 1 of 15 English-native respondents (or, within the smaller subset who answered the carpool-finding question, 17 of 21 vs. 1 of 2). While we frame this as a resonance with the literature rather than confirmation, the direction is consistent: messaging-based coordination is an active, everyday practice among international students.

第二步——缺口所要求的是整合平台。针对海外学生及相邻移动消息情境的研究记录了：微信、WhatsApp 等日常平台已经承载着跨境的实际协调、风险管理与求助行为 [Liu2023, Shi2018, Jin2021]。中国留学生在约束条件下组装「流动性技能库」（mobility repertoires），调动分布式社会关系以获取信息、情感支持与后勤协调 [Liu2023]。移动即时消息被记录为支撑非正式问题解决与知识交换的跨边界基础设施 [MobileIM2018]；当正式平台留下协调缺口时，私密消息群会扩展出互助、抗争与实践共同体（community of practice）等功能 [Bonini2023]。在我们的形成性调查中，72 名普通话母语受访者中有 17 人报告使用微信或 WhatsApp 群寻找拼车伙伴，而 15 名英语母语受访者中仅 1 人（或者，在回答了「如何寻找拼车」这一问题的较小子集内，为 17/21 对 1/2）。我们将其框定为与文献的「共鸣」而非「证实」，但方向是一致的：基于消息群的协调是国际学生中活跃的日常实践。

This coordination is not limited to rides. The same WeChat groups serve as channels for secondhand trading, event announcements, information sharing, and emergency assistance [You2023, Chen2024wechat, ShamirTixell2022]. Students already depend on a single social channel for multi-domain coordination, providing an experiential anchor for the legitimacy of a multi-module platform: the integration the design proposes is one that users already improvise informally.

这种协调并不限于拼车。同样的微信群也充当二手交易、活动公告、信息分享与紧急援助的渠道 [You2023, Chen2024wechat, ShamirTixell2022]。学生已经依赖单一社交渠道进行多领域协调，这为多模块平台的正当性提供了经验性锚点：设计所提议的整合，正是用户早已在非正式地即兴实践的。

A smaller body of super-app research indicates an emerging design space for closed-community integration within otherwise mass-market platforms. KakaoTalk chatrooms function as bounded “dwelling places” with primary, secondary, and tertiary social regions [Kim2015kakao]; LINE serves as intergenerational infrastructure inside Japanese family units [Ohashi2017line]; and nurse-facilitated patient groups inside WeChat operate as closed clinic-scoped coordination infrastructure [Wang2020wechat]. Platform-studies work on super-app platformization provides a theoretical backdrop [Steinberg2020line]. This literature indicates that bounded-scope multi-module coordination is a plausible design target, but a systematic treatment at the campus level is less developed—CampusRide contributes one case within this emerging space.

规模较小的「超级应用」（super-app）研究表明，在大众市场平台内部进行封闭社区整合是一个正在形成的设计空间。KakaoTalk 聊天室作为有边界的「栖居之所」（dwelling places）运作，呈现初级、次级与三级社会区域 [Kim2015kakao]；LINE 在日本家庭单元内部充当代际基础设施 [Ohashi2017line]；微信内由护士主持的患者群则作为限定于诊所范围的封闭协调基础设施运行 [Wang2020wechat]。关于超级应用平台化（platformization）的平台研究提供了理论背景 [Steinberg2020line]。这些文献表明，限定范围的多模块协调是一个可行的设计目标，但校园层面的系统性处理仍较欠缺——CampusRide 在这一新兴空间中贡献了一个案例。

Establishing the gap and the case for integration, however, does not settle how such a platform should work. A service gap by itself does not justify a peer platform: sharing-economy research shows that peer exchange becomes viable only when trust cues, participation incentives, and fairness perceptions are designed carefully [Ert2016, Blut2024, Broeder2023], with effects that differ by car ownership and user experience [Hartl2025] and that are platform- and context-dependent [Amrollahi2024]. Because most of this trust literature originates from Airbnb or general sharing-economy platforms, the case for a campus-scoped peer-rides system is motivated rather than fully established by prior work alone. We therefore distill, in §2.2, the four design primitives that operationalize these trust conditions for the campus setting.

然而，确立了缺口与整合的理由，并未解决这样一个平台应当如何运作。服务缺口本身并不足以论证一个点对点（peer-to-peer）平台的合理性：共享经济研究表明，唯有当信任线索、参与激励与公平感被精心设计时，点对点交换才可行 [Ert2016, Blut2024, Broeder2023]，且其效果因是否拥有汽车及用户经验而异 [Hartl2025]，并依赖于平台与情境 [Amrollahi2024]。由于现有信任文献大多源自 Airbnb 或一般共享经济平台，面向校园范围的点对点拼车系统，其论证是由先行工作「启发」（motivated）而非被其完全「确立」（established）的。因此，我们在 §2.2 中提炼出将这些信任条件落实到校园情境的四项设计基元。

2.2Design Primitives: Identity, Safety, Rating Fairness, and Rewards设计基元：身份、安全、评分公平性与奖励

We distill four design primitives from the literature, presenting them in parallel without value-ranking; the empirical findings in §4 and the design decisions in §5 perform the ranking work.

我们从文献中提炼四项设计基元，并以并列方式呈现、不做价值排序；§4 的实证发现与 §5 的设计决策承担排序工作。

Identity. Identity verification in sharing-economy contexts has a mature literature [Li2020trust, Ert2016]. We specialize this to institutional and .edu-scoped contexts, where closed-community scoping can reduce stranger-coordination costs. The foundational evidence comes from Facebook's .edu-gated era: college students' use of the then-closed network was positively associated with bridging social capital [Ellison2007], and institutionally seeded network ties enabled resource mobilization among college cohorts [Ellison2014]. Campus-bounded anonymous platforms such as Yik Yak further demonstrate that geographic or institutional scoping functions as a trust-relevant identity primitive even without explicit credential verification [Schlesinger2017, Black2016, Heston2016, Wu2017].

身份（Identity）。共享经济情境下的身份验证已有成熟文献 [Li2020trust, Ert2016]。我们将其特化到机构与 .edu 范围的情境：封闭社区的范围限定（scoping）可以降低陌生人协调成本。基础性证据来自 Facebook 的 .edu 门禁时代：大学生对当时这一封闭网络的使用与桥接型社会资本（bridging social capital）正相关 [Ellison2007]；由机构播种的网络关系使大学同辈群体得以进行资源动员 [Ellison2014]。Yik Yak 等以校园为边界的匿名平台进一步表明，即使没有显式的凭证验证，地理或机构范围限定本身就是一种与信任相关的身份基元 [Schlesinger2017, Black2016, Heston2016, Wu2017]。

Safety. Platforms can reduce uncertainty during a trip through real-time location sharing, SOS escalation, and shared situational awareness [Ali2024carpool, Divya2025]. A women-only immunization carpool in rural Pakistan demonstrated that transport coordination can be paired with explicit protective infrastructure [Ali2024carpool]. Peer ride-sharing design work emphasizes coordination and safety features over ratings, suggesting that amateur-driver settings may govern trust through different levers than professional gig platforms [RiderConnect2026].

安全（Safety）。平台可以通过实时位置共享、SOS 升级机制与共享态势感知来降低行程中的不确定性 [Ali2024carpool, Divya2025]。巴基斯坦农村一项面向免疫接种的女性专用拼车项目表明，交通协调可以与显式的保护性基础设施相配对 [Ali2024carpool]。点对点拼车设计研究强调协调与安全功能优先于评分，提示业余司机情境可能通过不同于职业零工平台的杠杆来治理信任 [RiderConnect2026]。

Rating fairness. Reputation systems coordinate trust, but recent work increasingly asks who is disadvantaged by biased feedback, asymmetric visibility, or platform-side promotion rules [Tushev2022, Christiaens2025]. Anti-discrimination design strategies document that sharing-economy platform features can trigger discrimination and that specific software design strategies can mitigate it [Tushev2022]. Platform-level fairness dynamics under biased ratings show that promotion policies can counteract rating-based discrimination [Smit2026]. Importantly, existing rating-fairness literature primarily covers professional gig workers; whether amateur or occasional drivers exhibit similar rating anxiety is a question we pursue in §4.2.

评分公平性（Rating fairness）。声誉系统（reputation system）协调信任，但近期研究越来越多地追问：谁会因有偏反馈、不对称可见性或平台侧推广规则而处于不利地位 [Tushev2022, Christiaens2025]。反歧视设计策略研究记录了共享经济平台功能可能诱发歧视、而特定软件设计策略可以缓解之 [Tushev2022]。在有偏评分条件下的平台级公平动力学研究显示，推广策略可以抵消基于评分的歧视 [Smit2026]。重要的是，现有评分公平性文献主要覆盖职业零工劳动者；业余或偶发性司机是否表现出类似的评分焦虑，正是我们在 §4.2 中追问的问题。

Rewards. Gamification is commonly discussed as a behavioral design layer that can sustain participation and pro-social mobility choices [Galeote2021, Zhang2023ant, Vacondio2025]. We treat it as a parallel lever with different behavioral aims than identity, safety, or fairness—not as a substitute for them.

奖励（Rewards）。游戏化（gamification）通常被讨论为一种行为设计层，可以维持参与并促进亲社会的出行选择 [Galeote2021, Zhang2023ant, Vacondio2025]。我们将其视为一根与身份、安全或公平性行为目标不同的并行杠杆——而非它们的替代品。

3Methodology研究方法

3.1Formative Survey Protocol形成性调查方案

We distributed an online survey via Qualtrics to students in the Cornell University and broader Ithaca area between February and March 2025. Participation was voluntary and anonymous; informed consent was obtained at the start of the survey. The survey yielded N=111 eligible respondents.

我们于 2025 年 2 月至 3 月通过 Qualtrics 向康奈尔大学及更广泛伊萨卡地区的学生发放在线问卷。参与自愿且匿名；问卷开始时获取知情同意（informed consent）。本次问卷获得 N=111 名合格受访者。

Among the 91 respondents who reported their native language, 72 (79%) indicated Mandarin Chinese, 15 (16%) indicated English, and 4 (5%) indicated other languages. We disclose this distribution as scope: our findings are most directly grounded in the experience of Mandarin-speaking international students, and we caution against generalizing to the broader campus population. Self-reported travel roles were: Rider (N=56), Driver (N=23), Both (N=22), and unreported (N=10).

Subset selection rationale. Q24 items are phrased as "As a driver, how tolerant would you be of…" Respondents who self-identified as Rider-only answered this question hypothetically, lacking actual driving experience to calibrate their responses. 子样本选择依据。Q24 条目的措辞为「作为司机，您对……的容忍度如何」。自我认定为 Rider-only（仅乘客）的受访者只是在假设情境下作答，缺乏真实驾驶经验来校准回答。将假设性回答与基于经验的回答混入同一分析，会稀释来自真实司机的信号。因此我们将 Q24 结果按两个子样本分别报告：(a) Driver/Both 子样本（各条目 N=17–19，其中完成全部四个条目的严格交集为 N=17），由自报为 Driver（司机）或 Both（两者皆是）的受访者组成；(b) Rider-only 对照组（各条目 N=11–12）。CHI 已发表研究中的众数样本量（modal sample size）为 N=12 [Caine2016]；我们 N=19 的描述性观察落在 CHI 形成性设计研究的本领域标准分布之内。

Literature dialogue. Algorithmic-management research has documented professional gig workers' anxiety toward platform rating systems [Keegan2025; Kinowska2022]. Our observation resonates with this concern in a smaller-scale, higher-trust, non-professional campus peer-carpool setting. Two implications follow. First, even with .edu identity verification as a strong trust signal, the fairness of the rating system itself remains an independent, non-substitutable design concern. Second, rating anxiety is not exclusively a product of professional-labor bargaining: a small but growing peer-to-peer carpool literature documents similar concerns among amateur drivers in carsharing and carpooling settings [Neifer2023; Hartl2025], and our Driver/Both-subset observation parallels this emerging pattern. Field experiments on two-sided reputation systems further document that the two sides of a peer marketplace respond asymmetrically to rating-system architecture [Fradkin2021], though those studies use an economic fairness framing distinct from our HCI design-research perspective. We treat this as a formative design signal, not a confirmed replication.

5.2Carpool Module拼车模块

The carpool module supports three distance categories: in-Ithaca commuting, short-distance regional trips, and long-distance trips to metropolitan areas (e.g., New York City, Boston, Syracuse Hancock Airport). It engages all four design primitives. The driver supply-willingness data (F6: long-distance 12/33 Very or Extremely willing, the highest of the three scenarios at 36%) directly motivated the design prioritization of long-distance trip matching as the primary use case.

拼车模块支持三类距离场景：伊萨卡市内通勤、短途区域行程，以及前往大都市区的长途行程（如纽约市、波士顿、雪城汉考克机场）。它运用了全部四个设计基元。司机供给意愿数据（F6：长途 12/33 选择「非常愿意」或「极其愿意」，以 36% 居三类场景之首）直接促成了将长途行程匹配作为首要用例的设计优先级。

State machine and booking flow. A ride row in the rides table moves through four states: active → full (when bookings fill all seats) → completed (driver-triggered) or cancelled (driver-triggered, soft-delete). A booking row in ride_bookings moves through confirmed (default, since we treat the demand-side as the decision point for this pilot) → cancelled. Seat availability is recomputed server-side on every booking and cancellation, rather than stored as a counter, so race conditions in the "last seat" case are narrow. Cancellation is disallowed after the scheduled departure_time. A ride cannot be updated once its status is completed or cancelled, and only the driver_id of a ride may trigger completeRide. These invariants are enforced at the controller layer, not via database constraints alone, so they are only as strong as the controller path.

状态机与预订流程。rides 表中的行程行经历四种状态：active（活跃）→ full（预订占满全部座位时）→ completed（司机触发完成）或 cancelled（司机触发取消，软删除，soft-delete）。ride_bookings 表中的预订行经历 confirmed（默认确认，因为本试点将需求侧视为决策点）→ cancelled。座位余量在每次预订与取消时都由服务端重新计算，而非存储为计数器，因此「最后一个座位」场景下的竞态条件（race condition）窗口很窄。计划的 departure_time（出发时间）之后不允许取消。状态为 completed 或 cancelled 的行程不可再更新，且只有该行程的 driver_id 可以触发 completeRide。这些不变量在控制器层而非仅靠数据库约束来强制执行，因此其可靠性取决于控制器路径本身。

Booking side-effects. A single successful booking triggers five side-effects in one request: (1) optional transition of the ride to full; (2) five notification records via notification.service (ride_new_booking, ride_booking_confirmed, ride_payment_confirmed, ride_payment_received, and two scheduled ride_rating_reminder rows whose data.showAfter field is departure_time + 2h); (3) automatic creation or membership-expansion of a ride_carpool group chat (detailed in §5.8.5); (4) a wxgroup_notice_record for an external WeChat notice channel; and (5) a Socket.IO event on the driver's personal room (user:{driver_id}) so the in-app badge updates in real time. Detailed design decisions follow in §5.8.

预订副作用。一次成功的预订在单个请求中触发五项副作用：(1) 视情况将行程转入 full 状态；(2) 经 notification.service 写入五条通知记录（ride_new_booking、ride_booking_confirmed、ride_payment_confirmed、ride_payment_received，以及两条计划的 ride_rating_reminder 行，其 data.showAfter 字段为 departure_time + 2h）；(3) 自动创建或扩充成员的 ride_carpool 群聊（详见 §5.8.5）；(4) 写入一条面向外部微信通知渠道的 wxgroup_notice_record；(5) 在司机个人房间（user:{driver_id}）上发出一个 Socket.IO 事件，使应用内角标实时更新。详细设计决策见 §5.8。

5.3Marketplace Module二手市场模块

The marketplace module enables peer-to-peer secondhand trading inside the .edu scope. A listing in marketplace_items carries seller_id, title, description, category, price, condition (one of new / like_new / good / fair / poor), location, a text[] of images, a text[] of tags, a life-cycle status (active / sold / removed), and aggregate counters views_count and favorites_count. Social surface comes from three companion tables: marketplace_comments (threaded comments with soft-delete), marketplace_comment_likes, and item_favorites. Deletion is implemented as a status = 'removed' soft-delete rather than row deletion, so a seller who retracts a listing leaves an auditable trail for the rating system and for cross-user conversation threads.

二手市场模块支持 .edu 范围内的点对点二手交易。marketplace_items 中的一条商品记录包含 seller_id（卖家 ID）、title（标题）、description（描述）、category（类别）、price（价格）、condition（成色，取值 new / like_new / good / fair / poor 之一）、location（地点）、text[] 类型的 images（图片数组）与 tags（标签数组）、生命周期 status（active / sold / removed），以及汇总计数器 views_count（浏览数）与 favorites_count（收藏数）。社交表层由三张配套表提供：marketplace_comments（支持软删除的层级评论）、marketplace_comment_likes（评论点赞）与 item_favorites（商品收藏）。删除实现为 status = 'removed' 的软删除而非物理删行，因此撤回商品的卖家会为评分系统与跨用户会话线程留下可审计的痕迹。

Cross-module reuse. Marketplace reuses three of the four carpool primitives. Identity: the .edu JWT is the shared admission gate, and the seller card on every listing carries the same verified-Cornell badge that drivers do. Messaging: clicking "Contact seller" opens a direct-message thread whose context_type = 'marketplace' and context_id = <item_id>, letting the recipient's inbox group messages by listing. Rewards: a successful transaction writes a marketplace_transaction point-transaction (base 8 points per the application-code catalog, Table 4), though in production this path has not yet written transactions.

跨模块复用。二手市场复用了拼车四基元中的三个。身份：.edu JWT（JSON Web Token，一种用于身份认证的令牌）是共享的准入门槛，每条商品的卖家卡片与司机一样展示同一枚「Cornell 已验证」徽章。消息：点击「联系卖家」会打开一个 context_type = 'marketplace'、context_id = <item_id> 的私信线程，使收件人的收件箱可按商品归组消息。奖励：一笔成功交易会写入一条 marketplace_transaction 积分流水（按应用代码目录基础分 8 分，见表 4），尽管在生产环境中该路径尚未写入过任何流水。

Outreach path. Every newly created listing fires a wxgroup_notice_record insert formatted as "二手上新 〈title〉 + short link," where the link is resolved by wechatLinkService.getBestNoticeLink—mini-program short link if available, H5 URL as fallback. In the 2026-04-23 snapshot this path accounts for 62 of 82 wxgroup_notice_record rows, making the marketplace the single largest producer of outbound WeChat pushes. The live 15-item inventory, all status = 'removed' with 12 aggregate views and 0 favorites (§5.9), shows the ingestion and cross-posting path works end-to-end even though organic buyer uptake has not yet followed.

外联路径。每条新建商品都会触发一条 wxgroup_notice_record 插入，格式为「二手上新 〈标题〉 + 短链接」，链接由 wechatLinkService.getBestNoticeLink 解析——优先小程序短链，回退为 H5 URL。在 2026-04-23 快照中，该路径占 82 条 wxgroup_notice_record 中的 62 条，使二手市场成为微信外推消息的最大单一来源。线上 15 件商品库存全部为 status = 'removed'，累计浏览 12 次、收藏 0 次（§5.9），表明录入与跨平台转发路径已端到端跑通，尽管自然买家的承接尚未跟上。

5.4Activities Module活动模块

The activities module supports event publication, registration, geo-verified check-in, and post-event rating. An activities row specifies a category chosen from {academic, sports, social, volunteer, career, cultural, technology}, a type from {individual, team, competition, workshop, seminar}, a location string paired with a location_coordinates JSONB, time bounds (start_time, end_time, registration_deadline), capacity (max_participants, current_participants), pricing (entry_fee cash, entry_fee_points cross-module pay-with-points), reward metadata (reward_points), a six-digit checkin_code, boolean location_verification, and a status machine {draft, published, ongoing, completed, cancelled}. A companion activity_participants row tracks each registration with attendance_status (registered / attended / cancelled / no_show), payment_status (pending / paid / refunded), points_earned, and a 1–5 rating field that the organizer populates on completion.

活动模块支持活动发布、报名、地理位置验证签到与赛后评分。一条 activities 记录指定：从 {academic（学术）, sports（体育）, social（社交）, volunteer（志愿）, career（职业）, cultural（文化）, technology（科技）} 中选取的 category；从 {individual（个人）, team（团队）, competition（竞赛）, workshop（工作坊）, seminar（研讨会）} 中选取的 type；与 location_coordinates JSONB（一种 PostgreSQL 二进制 JSON 数据类型）配对的 location 字符串；时间边界（start_time、end_time、registration_deadline）；容量（max_participants、current_participants）；定价（entry_fee 现金、entry_fee_points 跨模块积分支付）；奖励元数据（reward_points）；六位数 checkin_code（签到码）；布尔型 location_verification；以及状态机 {draft（草稿）, published（已发布）, ongoing（进行中）, completed（已完成）, cancelled（已取消）}。配套的 activity_participants 记录跟踪每次报名，含 attendance_status（出席状态：registered / attended / cancelled / no_show）、payment_status（支付状态：pending / paid / refunded）、points_earned（已获积分），以及由组织者在活动完成时填写的 1–5 分 rating 字段。

Counter consistency. current_participants is maintained by the update_activity_participant_count PL/pgSQL trigger, which increments on INSERT of a registered row and decrements when the attendance_status transitions out of registered, so the displayed seat count cannot diverge from the registration rows.

计数器一致性。current_participants 由 update_activity_participant_count 这一 PL/pgSQL 触发器（PostgreSQL 的过程化 SQL 语言触发器）维护：在 INSERT 一条 registered 行时递增，在 attendance_status 离开 registered 状态时递减，因此页面显示的名额数不会与报名记录发生偏离。

Geo-verified check-in. The check-in window opens checkin_start_offset minutes (default 30) before start_time and closes checkin_end_offset minutes (default 30) after end_time; the is_checkin_period Postgres function enforces this. Inside the window, a participant submits a location JSONB ({latitude, longitude, accuracy}); the calculate_distance function computes Haversine distance to activity.location_coordinates and marks location_verified = true if it falls within verification_radius (default 100 m). The full check-in row in activity_checkins additionally captures device_info JSONB (browser user-agent) and ip_address, so spoof attempts leave a forensic trail. A successful, geo-verified check-in is the trigger for the activity_checkin point-rule (5 points in the application-code catalog).

地理验证签到。签到窗口在 start_time 前 checkin_start_offset 分钟（默认 30）开启，在 end_time 后 checkin_end_offset 分钟（默认 30）关闭；由 Postgres 函数 is_checkin_period 强制执行。在窗口内，参与者提交位置 JSONB（{latitude, longitude, accuracy}）；calculate_distance 函数计算其与 activity.location_coordinates 之间的半正矢距离（Haversine distance，一种基于球面三角的两点大圆距离公式），若落在 verification_radius（默认 100 米）内则标记 location_verified = true。activity_checkins 中完整的签到记录还会采集 device_info JSONB（浏览器 user-agent）与 ip_address，使伪造尝试留下取证痕迹。一次成功且通过地理验证的签到是 activity_checkin 积分规则（应用代码目录中 5 分）的触发条件。

Cross-module reuse. Identity reuses the .edu JWT; messaging is embedded per-activity through activity_chat_messages and via DM threads scoped with context_type = 'activity'; rewards flow both directions (reward on completion, optional spend via entry_fee_points); the ride-style social channel is reused via automatic wxgroup_notice_record pushes on publication. In the 2026-04-23 snapshot the activities, activity_participants, activity_checkins, and activity_chat_messages tables all hold zero rows, but 4 wxgroup_notice_record activity pushes and 4 activity_registered / 4 activity_new_registration notifications (§5.9) attest to a short run of activity events in February 2026 whose transactional rows have since been cleaned.

跨模块复用。身份复用 .edu JWT；消息通过每活动一个的 activity_chat_messages 以及 context_type = 'activity' 范围的私信线程嵌入；奖励双向流动（完成时奖励，亦可经 entry_fee_points 消费）；行程式社交渠道经发布时自动写入的 wxgroup_notice_record 推送得到复用。在 2026-04-23 快照中，activities、activity_participants、activity_checkins 与 activity_chat_messages 四张表均为零行，但 4 条 wxgroup_notice_record 活动推送与 4 条 activity_registered／4 条 activity_new_registration 通知（§5.9）证明 2026 年 2 月曾有一小段活动运行，其事务性记录此后已被清理。

5.5Groups Module群组模块

The groups module provides two kinds of persistent social containers sharing one table. A groups row carries name, description, cover_image, creator_id, member_count, and a group_kind discriminator whose two current values are community (interest- or geography-based groups) and ride_carpool (trip-bound groups, §5.8.5); ride-bound rows additionally set ride_id and chat_expires_at. Membership lives in group_members with a role of creator or member.

群组模块以同一张表提供两类持久化社交容器。一条 groups 记录包含 name（名称）、description（描述）、cover_image（封面图）、creator_id（创建者 ID）、member_count（成员数），以及判别字段 group_kind，当前两个取值为 community（基于兴趣或地理的社区群组）与 ride_carpool（绑定行程的群组，§5.8.5）；行程绑定的记录额外设置 ride_id 与 chat_expires_at（群聊过期时间）。成员关系存于 group_members，role（角色）为 creator 或 member。

Messaging substrate. group_messages enforces a char_length(content) BETWEEN 1 AND 2000 check, a message_type enum of {text, image, file}, and a soft-delete column (deleted_at) so moderation actions do not rewrite history. Row-level security is non-trivial here: the group_messages_select_policy admits SELECT only to rows whose auth.uid() has a live group_members entry, and the insert policy additionally forces auth.uid() = sender_id, so the identity claim on every message is structurally rather than application-enforced. Update and delete policies are restricted to the original sender, and a server-side trigger keeps updated_at fresh.

消息基底。group_messages 强制 char_length(content) BETWEEN 1 AND 2000 检查、message_type 枚举 {text, image, file}，并设软删除列（deleted_at），使审核操作不会改写历史。此处的行级安全（Row-Level Security，RLS，即数据库按行粒度控制读写权限的机制）并不平凡：group_messages_select_policy 仅向 auth.uid() 在 group_members 中拥有有效条目的行开放 SELECT，插入策略还强制 auth.uid() = sender_id，因此每条消息上的身份声明是由数据库结构而非应用层来保证的。更新与删除策略仅限原发送者，服务端触发器持续刷新 updated_at。

Moderation (migration 010). A group creator or a platform moderator can mute a member through group_muted_users (muted_by, optional reason, muted_at, unmuted_at) and can retract a message through group_message_deletions (which pairs with the soft-delete fields is_deleted, deleted_at, deleted_by on group_messages). These affordances exist for community groups but, as §5.8.5 notes, are not yet wired into the ride-scoped ride_carpool rooms.

审核机制（迁移脚本 010）。群组创建者或平台审核员可通过 group_muted_users（muted_by、可选 reason、muted_at、unmuted_at）禁言成员，并可通过 group_message_deletions 撤回消息（与 group_messages 上的软删除字段 is_deleted、deleted_at、deleted_by 配对）。这些能力对社区群组可用，但如 §5.8.5 所述，尚未接入按行程划定范围的 ride_carpool 房间。

System groups and discovery. Migration 008 provisions two system-level rooms with fixed UUIDs—a platform-wide Carpooling room (00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000001) and a Marketplace room (...002)—that all users can reach without joining. A group-map view atop cover_image + optional geography furnishes the social-discovery layer. In the 2026-04-23 snapshot, groups holds 5 community rows, all with member_count = 1 (creator-only), suggesting that group creation is working but that the discovery-to-join funnel has not yet had its flywheel moment.

系统群组与发现。迁移脚本 008 预置了两个固定 UUID 的系统级房间——平台级拼车房间（00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000001）与二手市场房间（...002）——所有用户无需加入即可访问。基于 cover_image 与可选地理信息的群组地图视图提供了社交发现层。在 2026-04-23 快照中，groups 含 5 条 community 记录，全部 member_count = 1（仅创建者），表明群组创建功能正常，但「发现到加入」的漏斗尚未迎来其飞轮时刻（flywheel moment，指增长进入自我强化循环的临界点）。

5.6Messages Module消息模块

The messaging module carries three distinct surfaces on a shared Socket.IO substrate: direct messages (messages), group chat (group_messages, §5.5), and system messages (system_messages, including a user-submitted-feedback channel).

消息模块在共享的 Socket.IO 基底上承载三个不同的界面层：私信（messages）、群聊（group_messages，§5.5）与系统消息（system_messages，含用户提交反馈的渠道）。

Direct-message schema. A messages row records sender_id, receiver_id, subject (2–255 chars), content (1–2000 chars), and four typing dimensions: message_type (general / activity_inquiry / activity_update / support), context_type and context_id which cross-link a message to its originating object (a ride, a listing, an activity), priority (low / normal / high), and thread_id plus reply_to for threading. The companion message_participants row represents each side's view (read status, archive flag) so that one user's archival does not mutate the counterparty's inbox.

私信数据结构。一条 messages 记录包含 sender_id（发送者）、receiver_id（接收者）、subject（主题，2–255 字符）、content（正文，1–2000 字符），以及四个类型维度：message_type（general / activity_inquiry / activity_update / support）；将消息与其源对象（一次行程、一件商品、一场活动）交叉关联的 context_type 与 context_id；priority（low / normal / high）；以及用于串联会话的 thread_id 与 reply_to。配套的 message_participants 记录表示双方各自的视图（已读状态、归档标记），因此一方的归档不会改动对方的收件箱。

Reply-required guard against cold-message spam. The messageService.sendMessage path enforces a REPLY_REQUIRED rule: once a user has sent a direct message to a recipient they have no prior thread with, subsequent messages are refused with HTTP 403 until the recipient replies at least once. This is a platform-side mitigation for unsolicited mass DMs; it is a light-touch analog of the first-message-friction pattern often discussed in sharing-economy safety literature.

防陌生消息骚扰的「须回复」守卫。messageService.sendMessage 路径强制执行 REPLY_REQUIRED 规则：用户向无既往会话的接收者发出一条私信后，后续消息将被以 HTTP 403 拒绝，直至接收者至少回复一次。这是平台侧对未经同意的批量私信的缓解措施，是共享经济安全文献中常讨论的「首条消息摩擦」（first-message friction）模式的轻量类比。

System messages and the feedback channel. system_messages carries a sender_type of admin (pinned announcements) or user (submitted feedback). Each row optionally pins (is_pinned) and typologizes via message_type. In the 2026-04-23 snapshot all 10 rows have sender_type = 'user' and message_type = 'feedback', dated 2026-01-10 to 2026-02-11; no admin announcements have been posted yet, meaning the user-feedback subsurface is, in practice, the entire current use of the system-messages surface.

系统消息与反馈渠道。system_messages 的 sender_type 取值为 admin（置顶公告）或 user（用户提交的反馈）。每条记录可选置顶（is_pinned），并经 message_type 归类。在 2026-04-23 快照中，全部 10 条记录均为 sender_type = 'user' 且 message_type = 'feedback'，日期为 2026-01-10 至 2026-02-11；尚无管理员公告发布，意味着用户反馈这一子界面实际上就是系统消息界面当前的全部用途。

Real-time substrate. Socket.IO rooms namespace three kinds of live state: user:{userId} for personal notifications and presence, activity:{activityId} for activity chat, and thread:{threadId} for DM and group-message typing indicators. JWT authentication on connect is mandatory; guests receive a limited type='guest' token. The typing_indicator event is broadcast to the thread room excluding the sender, matching the UX idiom users expect from WeChat.

实时基底。Socket.IO 房间为三类实时状态划分命名空间：user:{userId} 用于个人通知与在线状态，activity:{activityId} 用于活动聊天，thread:{threadId} 用于私信与群消息的「正在输入」指示。连接时的 JWT 认证是强制的；访客获得受限的 type='guest' 令牌。typing_indicator 事件向线程房间内除发送者之外的成员广播，符合用户从微信习得的交互习惯。

Cross-module embedding. Messaging is deeply embedded with every other module: a carpool booking materializes a ride_carpool group (§5.8.5); a marketplace "Contact seller" opens a context_type='marketplace' DM; activities carry both a group chat and DM threads with context_type='activity'. This embedding is what the Introduction means by "multi-module"—the modules are not simply co-installed apps but share the same conversational substrate. Live data (§5.9): 22 DMs, all message_type='general' and all is_read=true, concentrated in a 3-week burst around 2026-02-05 to 2026-02-28; plus 18 message_reply notifications attesting to that burst.

跨模块嵌入。消息与其他每个模块都深度嵌合：一次拼车预订会实体化一个 ride_carpool 群组（§5.8.5）；二手市场的「联系卖家」会打开 context_type='marketplace' 的私信；活动同时拥有群聊与 context_type='activity' 的私信线程。这种嵌入正是引言中「多模块」一词的含义——各模块并非简单并装的应用，而是共享同一个会话基底。实际数据（§5.9）：22 条私信，全部为 message_type='general' 且 is_read=true，集中于约 2026-02-05 至 2026-02-28 的三周爆发期；另有 18 条 message_reply 通知佐证这一爆发。

5.7Points Module (Cross-Module Gamification)积分模块（跨模块游戏化）

The points module is a cross-module meta-layer that treats every other module as both a source and a sink of points. Sources: registration, email verification, profile completion, daily login, ride creation and completion, marketplace transaction, activity creation / organization / participation / check-in / completion, and a consecutive-check-in streak bonus (the only rule that honors the multiplier field). Sinks: a coupons table (currently empty) for redemption rewards, and the activities.entry_fee_points field, which lets an organizer charge an activity in points rather than cash. A single awardPoints service writes a row to point_transactions with rule_type, source, reason, metadata, and multiplier, and atomically increments users.points via the increment_user_points RPC; a symmetric deductPoints refuses to go below zero. Design rationale and the full rule catalog are in §5.8.4.

积分模块是一个跨模块元层，将其余每个模块同时视为积分的「来源」（source）与「去向」（sink）。来源包括：注册、邮箱验证、完善资料、每日登录、行程创建与完成、二手交易、活动的创建／组织／参与／签到／完成，以及连续签到奖励（唯一遵循 multiplier 字段的规则）。去向包括：用于兑换奖励的 coupons 表（当前为空），以及允许组织者以积分而非现金收取活动费用的 activities.entry_fee_points 字段。单一的 awardPoints 服务向 point_transactions 写入一条含 rule_type、source、reason、metadata 与 multiplier 的记录，并经 increment_user_points RPC（远程过程调用，Remote Procedure Call）原子地递增 users.points；对称的 deductPoints 拒绝使余额低于零。设计依据与完整规则目录见 §5.8.4。

Why secondary by design. The motivation data (F4: gamification at 48.3, the second tier behind financial motivation at 63.6) informed a deliberate design choice: base-point payouts are capped well below the cash value of the corresponding transaction (e.g., 15 points for a completed ride whose per-seat price is typically $22–$38), so points complement rather than substitute the cash lever. This avoids the algorithmic-management failure mode where a gamified point economy becomes the dominant motivator and then warps participation into point-chasing (§7.2).

为何刻意置于次要位置。动机数据（F4：游戏化 48.3，居经济动机 63.6 之后的第二层级）促成了一个刻意的设计选择：基础积分的发放被限定在远低于对应交易现金价值的水平（例如完成一次行程得 15 分，而其每座价格通常为 $22–$38），使积分成为现金杠杆的补充而非替代。这避免了算法管理的失效模式——游戏化积分经济成为主导动机，进而将参与扭曲为「刷分」（point-chasing）（§7.2）。

Deployment status caveat. The points subsystem is present in code but not yet provisioned in the Railway-backed production database: point_rules has zero rows, point_transactions does not exist as a table, and every one of the 184 live user rows carries users.points = 0. The design is therefore implementation-ready but behaviorally inert; we flag this in §5.8.4 and §5.9.

部署状态提醒。积分子系统存在于代码中，但「尚未在 Railway 托管的生产数据库中开通」：point_rules 为零行，point_transactions 作为表并不存在，且 184 条线上用户记录的 users.points 均为 0。因此该设计处于实现就绪但行为上未生效的状态；我们在 §5.8.4 与 §5.9 中如实标注。

5.8Carpool Deep-Dive: Five Design Decisions拼车深入剖析：五项设计决策

Each design decision below is explicitly motivated by a survey finding, and each is described against the implemented controller / service / schema that backs it in the deployed system (§5.9). Decisions 1–4 correspond to the four design primitives distilled in §2.2; decision 5 documents the cross-module integration lever that makes the multi-module claim operational at the ride level.

以下每项设计决策都明确由某一调查发现所驱动，且都对照已部署系统（§5.9）中支撑它的实际控制器（controller）／服务（service）／数据结构（schema）加以描述。决策 1–4 对应 §2.2 提炼的四个设计基元；决策 5 记录了使「多模块」主张在行程层面得以落地的跨模块整合杠杆。

5.8.1Identity Verification身份验证

Design decision. .edu email verification as a registration gate and a persistent trust signal displayed on driver and passenger profiles.

设计决策。以 .edu 邮箱验证作为注册门槛，并作为持久信任信号展示在司机与乘客的个人资料上。

Evidence. F3 shows .edu verification has a mean WTP uplift of 67.3 (median 79), the second-highest of the seven safety features.

证据。F3 显示 .edu 验证的支付意愿提升均值为 67.3（中位数 79），在七项安全功能中居第二。

Implementation. New users supply an email, password, first_name, last_name, and student_id; the backend generates an email_verification_token (and email_verification_expires) and sends a verification mail via Resend. Clicking the link flips the row's is_verified flag to true. A second, stricter flag, verification_status (values pending / verified), is reserved for future manual-review workflows but currently transitions in lockstep with is_verified because the manual-review step is not yet instantiated. The Socket.IO authentication middleware accepts only a JWT signed with the Cornell-scoped user row; guests receive a separate type='guest' token with read-only capabilities. The verified badge and university string ("Cornell University") are embedded in every driver and passenger card returned by getRides and getRideById.

实现。新用户提供 email、password、first_name、last_name 与 student_id；后端生成 email_verification_token（及 email_verification_expires）并经 Resend（一个事务性邮件发送服务）发送验证邮件。点击链接后将该行的 is_verified 标志翻转为 true。第二个更严格的标志 verification_status（取值 pending / verified）为未来的人工审核流程预留，但由于人工审核环节尚未建立，目前与 is_verified 同步流转。Socket.IO 认证中间件只接受以 Cornell 范围用户行签名的 JWT；访客获得单独的 type='guest' 只读令牌。已验证徽章与 university 字符串（"Cornell University"）嵌入 getRides 与 getRideById 返回的每张司机与乘客卡片中。

Limitation. .edu verification confirms institutional affiliation but does not verify driving competence, behavioral reliability, or insurance status. The pilot observation on the Railway-backed production database (§5.9) shows 170 of 184 user rows with is_verified = true and an identical count in verification_status = 'verified', confirming that the .edu token loop is self-consistent and that 100% of rows carry a @cornell.edu address, but also that the two-tier model collapses to a one-tier gate in practice because no manual-review queue is running.

局限。.edu 验证确认的是机构隶属关系，并不验证驾驶能力、行为可靠性或保险状况。对 Railway 托管生产数据库（§5.9）的试点观察显示：184 条用户记录中 170 条 is_verified = true，且 verification_status = 'verified' 的数量与之完全一致，证实 .edu 令牌闭环自洽、100% 的记录持有 @cornell.edu 地址；但也表明由于没有人工审核队列在运行，两级模型在实践中坍缩为一级门槛。

5.8.2Safety Skeleton安全骨架

Design decision. Real-time location sharing and a one-tap SOS button as two active safety layers during trips.

设计决策。以实时位置共享与一键 SOS 按钮作为行程期间的两道主动安全防线。

Evidence. F3 shows real-time location sharing as the highest-WTP feature (mean 69.1, median 76) and Emergency SOS as the third-highest (mean 63.5, median 64).

证据。F3 显示实时位置共享是支付意愿最高的功能（均值 69.1，中位数 76），紧急 SOS 居第三（均值 63.5，中位数 64）。

Implementation. Each ride booking admits its driver and passenger to a Socket.IO room namespaced as thread:{thread_id} and, in parallel, to the auto-created ride-scoped group chat room (§5.8.5). Typing indicators, online presence, and position updates are broadcast inside these rooms only. The SOS button triggers a high-priority notification row with priority = 'high' targeted at pre-configured emergency contacts and the current passenger set, plus an immediate Socket.IO emit on each recipient's user:{userId} room; device context (browser user-agent, IP, geolocation accuracy) is captured in the activity check-in schema's device_info JSONB column and is reused for the rideshare SOS path. Activities that opt in to location-gated check-in compute Haversine distance between the user location JSONB ({latitude, longitude, accuracy}) and the activity's coordinates using the calculate_distance Postgres function, with a configurable verification_radius (default 100 m); the same primitive is available to the rideshare module for pickup-point verification.

实现。每次行程预订都将司机与乘客接入命名空间为 thread:{thread_id} 的 Socket.IO 房间，并同时接入自动创建的行程群聊房间（§5.8.5）。「正在输入」指示、在线状态与位置更新仅在这些房间内部广播。SOS 按钮触发一条 priority = 'high' 的高优先级 notification 记录，目标为预先配置的紧急联系人与当前乘客集合，并立即在每位接收者的 user:{userId} 房间上发出 Socket.IO 事件；设备上下文（浏览器 user-agent、IP、地理定位精度）采集于活动签到结构的 device_info JSONB 列，并被拼车 SOS 路径复用。启用位置门控签到的活动会使用 calculate_distance Postgres 函数计算用户位置 JSONB（{latitude, longitude, accuracy}）与活动坐标之间的半正矢距离（Haversine distance），配以可配置的 verification_radius（默认 100 米）；同一基元也可供拼车模块用于上车点验证。

Limitation. Location sharing creates a privacy trade-off that the current implementation resolves by room-scoping rather than by retention policy—raw coordinates are not persisted beyond the active session but also are not redacted in ride-group chat messages. SOS response chains depend on resources outside the platform, and the pilot has recorded zero SOS activations, so the end-to-end latency of the SOS path is currently uninstrumented.

局限。位置共享带来隐私权衡，当前实现以房间限定范围而非数据保留策略来化解——原始坐标不会在活跃会话之外持久化，但在行程群聊消息中也未做脱敏。SOS 响应链依赖平台之外的资源，且试点期间 SOS 激活次数为零，因此 SOS 路径的端到端时延目前没有任何实测数据。

5.8.3Rating System with Fairness Consideration兼顾公平性的评分系统

Design decision. Bidirectional (passenger ↔ driver) ratings on a 1–5 integer scale with optional free-text comments; a deferred rating-readiness window that opens 2 hours after departure_time (or at status = 'completed') rather than at booking; role-paired rating so that only the trip's driver may rate its passengers and vice versa; and self-revision of one's own rating via update-in-place until subsequent system-level mechanisms retire the row.

设计决策。双向（乘客 ↔ 司机）的 1–5 整数评分并附可选自由文本评论；延迟的「可评分」窗口在 departure_time 之后 2 小时（或 status = 'completed' 时）开启，而非在预订时即可评分；角色配对评分，使只有该行程的司机能评价其乘客，反之亦然；以及在后续系统级机制使记录退役之前，可通过原地更新（update-in-place）自行修订自己的评分。

Evidence. F5, limited to the Driver/Both subset (N=19): "unfair rating" tolerance at 29.1 is 12.3–23.2 points below the other three items (41.4–52.3), indicating that rating-system fairness is the most salient concern for respondents with driving experience.

证据。F5，限于 Driver/Both 子样本（N=19）：「不公平评分」容忍度 29.1 比其余三项（41.4–52.3）低 12.3–23.2 分，表明评分系统公平性是有驾驶经验受访者最突出的关切。

Implementation. The ratings table keys on (trip_id, rater_id, ratee_id) and records role_of_rater ('driver' or 'passenger'), score (CHECK 1–5), comment, and timestamps. The createRating controller refuses self-ratings, verifies that the rater is either the ride's driver or a non-cancelled passenger via a ride_bookings lookup, enforces that drivers may only rate their passengers (and vice versa), and blocks submission until Date.now() >= departure_time + 2h (constant RATING_READY_DELAY_MS). An existing rating row is overwritten rather than appended, and the user-level users.avg_rating / users.total_ratings cache is recomputed as an unweighted mean across the union of ratings and activity_ratings rows for the ratee. A pair of ride_rating_reminder notifications with data.showAfter = departure + 2h is queued at booking time so both sides see a tap-to-rate card in their message inbox once the window opens.

实现。ratings 表以 (trip_id, rater_id, ratee_id) 为键，记录 role_of_rater（'driver' 或 'passenger'）、score（CHECK 约束 1–5）、comment 与时间戳。createRating 控制器拒绝自评，通过 ride_bookings 查询验证评分者是该行程的司机或未取消的乘客，强制司机只能评价其乘客（反之亦然），并在 Date.now() >= departure_time + 2h（常量 RATING_READY_DELAY_MS）之前阻止提交。已存在的评分记录会被覆盖而非追加，用户级的 users.avg_rating／users.total_ratings 缓存则以被评者在 ratings 与 activity_ratings 两表并集上的无加权均值重新计算。预订时即排入一对 data.showAfter = departure + 2h 的 ride_rating_reminder 通知，使窗口开启后双方都能在消息收件箱看到「点按评分」卡片。

Design rationale. Field experiments on two-sided reputation systems [Fradkin2021] show that architectural choices (e.g., hiding feedback until both parties submit) change rating behavior. The 2-hour readiness delay is a simpler variant of this architectural idea: it removes the in-vehicle moment from the rating submission path, reducing the most immediate retaliation surface. The role-pairing and self-revision primitives draw on CSCW design-case work on algorithm-based reputation systems for peer-to-peer carsharing [Neifer2023].

设计依据。关于双边声誉系统的现场实验 [Fradkin2021] 表明，架构选择（例如在双方都提交之前隐藏反馈）会改变评分行为。2 小时就绪延迟是这一架构思想的简化变体：它将「车内时刻」移出评分提交路径，削减了最直接的报复面。角色配对与自我修订两个基元借鉴了 CSCW（计算机支持的协同工作，Computer-Supported Cooperative Work）领域关于点对点共享汽车算法声誉系统的设计案例研究 [Neifer2023]。

Scope of the implemented design. Three mechanisms that we considered—a formal pre-publication dispute window, cross-trip trend protection for a single anomalous rating, and mandatory rating justifications—are not yet implemented. Update-in-place is the only present-day revision affordance and does not bind either party to a fixed deliberation period. We retain the other three as open design proposals.

已实现设计的范围。我们曾考虑的三种机制——正式的发布前争议窗口、针对单次异常评分的跨行程趋势保护、强制评分理由——均「尚未」实现。原地更新是当下唯一的修订手段，且不将任何一方约束在固定的斟酌期内。其余三项保留为开放设计提案。

Limitation. Update-in-place allows a disgruntled rater to revise downward at any time, which is the opposite failure mode from the dispute-window design. Simple-mean aggregation provides no outlier smoothing, so the first negative rating dominates the displayed avg_rating for a new driver. And because ratings currently contains zero rows in the live database (§5.9), whether any of these mechanisms alleviate driver rating anxiety cannot be assessed without sustained usage.

局限。原地更新允许心怀不满的评分者随时下调评分，这是与争议窗口设计相反的失效模式。简单均值聚合不提供异常值平滑，因此第一条差评会主导新司机展示的 avg_rating。而由于线上数据库中 ratings 当前为零行（§5.9），这些机制能否缓解司机的评分焦虑，在缺乏持续使用之前无从评估。

5.8.4Gamification as Secondary Incentive作为次级激励的游戏化

Design decision. Points serve as a secondary incentive layer, deliberately not dominating the economic motivation.

设计决策。积分作为次级激励层，刻意不凌驾于经济动机之上。

Evidence. F4 places gamification (48.3) in a second tier behind financial motivation (63.6).

证据。F4 将游戏化（48.3）置于经济动机（63.6）之后的第二层级。

Implementation. All point-affecting events route through a single awardPoints service that writes a row to point_transactions (user_id, rule_type, points, source, reason, metadata, multiplier, created_at) and atomically increments users.points via the increment_user_points RPC. The application-code rule catalog (Table 4) sets registration = 10, verification = 5, daily_login = 1, profile_complete = 15, and a set of activity-triggered rewards (activity_creation = 15, activity_participation = 10, activity_organization = 30, activity_checkin = 5, activity_completion = 15); the points.service additionally defines rideshare_completion = 15, marketplace_transaction = 8, referral = 30, and a consecutive_checkin streak rule whose multiplier attribute is respected by awardPoints. A deductPoints counterpart refuses to go below zero (returning INSUFFICIENT_POINTS) so points are kept non-negative. The base-point schedule is deliberately capped below the per-seat cash price of a typical long-distance ride, so points remain a complement to, not a substitute for, cash.

实现。所有影响积分的事件都经由单一的 awardPoints 服务路由：它向 point_transactions 写入一条记录（user_id、rule_type、points、source、reason、metadata、multiplier、created_at），并经 increment_user_points RPC 原子递增 users.points。应用代码规则目录（表 4）设定 registration = 10、verification = 5、daily_login = 1、profile_complete = 15，以及一组活动触发奖励（activity_creation = 15、activity_participation = 10、activity_organization = 30、activity_checkin = 5、activity_completion = 15）；points.service 还定义了 rideshare_completion = 15、marketplace_transaction = 8、referral = 30，以及 multiplier 属性会被 awardPoints 遵循的 consecutive_checkin 连续签到规则。对应的 deductPoints 拒绝使余额低于零（返回 INSUFFICIENT_POINTS），从而保持积分非负。基础积分表刻意限定在典型长途行程每座现金价格之下，使积分始终是现金的补充而非替代。

Limitation. Cross-module points may incentivize gaming behavior such as fake trips, collusive check-ins, or strategic compliance; the only present anti-gaming lever is the validateActivityPoints stub, which returns {valid: true} and is explicitly annotated as a placeholder for future rate-limit logic. 5.8.5Messaging Embedding: Ride-Scoped Group Chat消息嵌入：按行程划定的群聊

Design decision. Every carpool booking materializes a ride-scoped group chat, so communication follows the trip rather than the individuals. The chat is automatically created on first booking, auto-expires one hour after the scheduled departure, and tracks membership against live bookings.

设计决策。每次拼车预订都会实体化一个按行程划定的群聊，使沟通跟随行程而非跟随个人。群聊在首次预订时自动创建，在计划出发后一小时自动过期，并依据有效预订追踪成员关系。

Evidence. The grassroots-practice literature we review in §2.1 documents that students already run ride coordination inside persistent WeChat / WhatsApp groups [Liu2023; Bonini2023]. Our survey observation that 17 of 21 Mandarin-native respondents who answered the carpool-finding question used such groups resonates with that pattern.

证据。我们在 §2.1 综述的草根实践文献记录了学生本就在持久的微信／WhatsApp 群中开展拼车协调 [Liu2023; Bonini2023]。我们的调查观察——回答「如何寻找拼车伙伴」问题的 21 名普通话母语受访者中有 17 名使用此类群组——与该模式形成共鸣。

Implementation. The ensureRideCarpoolGroupOnBooking service, called inline from bookRide, looks up any existing groups row with the booking's ride_id. If none exists, it inserts one with group_kind = 'ride_carpool', name = 'Ride: <title>', creator_id = driver_id, and chat_expires_at = departure_time + 1h. The driver is inserted into group_members with role = 'creator' and the passenger with role = 'member' (duplicate-key collisions on Postgres code 23505 are silently swallowed to make the call idempotent). On passenger cancellation, removePassengerFromRideGroup deletes their group_members row while leaving the group intact for remaining riders. A subsequent updateRide that changes departure_time calls syncRideCarpoolGroupExpiry, which re-computes and writes chat_expires_at. Messages inside these rooms flow through the same Socket.IO thread-room primitive as direct messages, so the typing indicator and read-receipt affordances are shared across module boundaries. Row-level security on group_messages restricts SELECT to current group members and INSERT to current members posting as themselves.

实现。由 bookRide 内联调用的 ensureRideCarpoolGroupOnBooking 服务会查找与该预订 ride_id 对应的现有 groups 记录。若不存在，则插入一条 group_kind = 'ride_carpool'、name = 'Ride: <title>'、creator_id = driver_id、chat_expires_at = departure_time + 1h 的记录。司机以 role = 'creator'、乘客以 role = 'member' 插入 group_members（Postgres 错误码 23505 的重复键冲突被静默吞掉，以使调用幂等，idempotent，即重复执行不产生额外效果）。乘客取消时，removePassengerFromRideGroup 删除其 group_members 记录，而群组为其余乘客保持完好。随后改变 departure_time 的 updateRide 会调用 syncRideCarpoolGroupExpiry，重新计算并写入 chat_expires_at。这些房间内的消息与私信共用同一 Socket.IO 线程房间基元，因此「正在输入」指示与已读回执能力跨模块边界共享。group_messages 上的行级安全将 SELECT 限于当前群成员，将 INSERT 限于以本人身份发帖的当前成员。

Limitation. The one-hour post-departure expiry matches the "trip-bound" framing of the module but forecloses post-trip reconciliation chat (for lost items, fare corrections, or trailing rating disputes) unless one party re-opens the thread as a direct message. Ride-scoped chats do not inherit mute or moderation state from the system-level moderation tables added for community groups in migration 010, so a misbehaving co-rider cannot be muted inside the ride chat without kicking them. In the Railway-backed production database no group_kind = 'ride_carpool' rows are currently live (all five extant groups are community); the 82 ride-related push records in wxgroup_notice_record and the 8 queued ride_rating_reminder notifications attest to historical ride bookings that pre-date the current rides table state (§5.9).

局限。出发后一小时过期与模块「行程绑定」的定位相符，却也封闭了行后善后沟通（失物、车费更正、后续评分争议）的通道，除非一方以私信重新打开会话。行程群聊未继承迁移脚本 010 为社区群组新增的系统级审核表中的禁言或审核状态，因此行为不当的同行乘客无法在行程群聊内被禁言，只能将其移出。在 Railway 托管的生产数据库中，目前没有任何 group_kind = 'ride_carpool' 的有效记录（现存五个群组全部为 community）；wxgroup_notice_record 中的 82 条行程相关推送记录与 8 条排队中的 ride_rating_reminder 通知，佐证了早于当前 rides 表状态的历史行程预订（§5.9）。

6Research-Agent Pipeline as Methodology Contribution作为方法学贡献的 Research-Agent 流水线

6.1Architecture and Eight-Phase Flow架构与八阶段流程

The pipeline uses a provider layer (OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, arXiv, Lens, Crossref), a reasoning layer (Claude and GPT models tiered by task complexity), an identity layer (DOI-based canonical IDs), a state machine (phase-based resumability), and a contract layer enforcing structural invariants across phases. The eight phases are: (1) Corpus Assembly, (2) Classification into a ten-category taxonomy (A–J), (2.5) Deep Extraction of claims, methods, and findings, (3) Relationship Graph with evidence-sufficiency diagnostics, (3.5) Narrative Chain generation per beat, (3.7) Contradiction Map, (4) Evidence Inventory, and (5) Five-Reviewer Evaluation (narrative, coverage, gap, contradiction, and honesty reviewers, each scoring independently).

该流水线包含：数据源层（provider layer：OpenAlex、Semantic Scholar、arXiv、Lens、Crossref 五个学术文献数据库）、推理层（reasoning layer：按任务复杂度分级调用 Claude 与 GPT 模型）、标识层（identity layer：基于 DOI——数字对象唯一标识符——的规范 ID）、状态机（state machine：支持按阶段断点续跑）以及契约层（contract layer：跨阶段强制执行结构不变量）。八个阶段为：(1) 语料组装（Corpus Assembly）；(2) 按 A–J 十类分类法分类；(2.5) 对论断、方法与发现的深度抽取（Deep Extraction）；(3) 带证据充分性诊断（evidence-sufficiency diagnostics）的关系图谱（Relationship Graph）；(3.5) 按叙事节拍生成叙事链（Narrative Chain）；(3.7) 矛盾图谱（Contradiction Map）；(4) 证据清单（Evidence Inventory）；(5) 五评审人评估（Five-Reviewer Evaluation：叙事、覆盖度、缺口、矛盾与诚实性五位评审各自独立打分）。

The subsection was rewritten to match the deployed mechanism and to flag the dispute-window / trend-protection ideas as open design proposals—an alignment that the pipeline, which works abstract-first over prior literature rather than over the codebase, does not enforce. Fourth, a first draft of §5.9 reported numbers from the repo's .env.production Supabase project (jfgenxnqpuutgdnnngsl), which turned out to be a superseded staging / demo database seeded with showcase content (one demo driver, 8 fabricated rides, a point_transactions table populated by registration bonuses). 6.3Scope and Honest Limitations of the Pipeline流水线的范围与诚实局限

The pipeline is not an open-ended research agent, an automated thesis generator, or a gap-discovery tool (its gaps_ranked.json output is a diagnostic signal, not a conclusion). It operates abstract-first and does not perform full-text truth extraction. It does not perform subset robustness checks on primary survey data, requiring human intervention for analyses such as the Driver/Both vs. Rider-only split reported in §4.2. It also does not cross-check design claims in the manuscript against the implemented codebase or deployed schema—the mechanism correction in §5.8.3 was performed manually by inspecting rating.controller.js, the ratings table DDL, and the live point_rules catalog, and is recorded as the third negative-audit instance above. We recommend that future users treat it as a structuring and auditing complement to human judgment, not as a replacement, and that design-case papers pair it with an explicit implementation-versus-manuscript reconciliation pass.

该流水线不是开放式研究智能体、自动论文生成器或缺口发现工具（其 gaps_ranked.json 输出是诊断信号，不是结论）。它以摘要为先运作，不做全文真值抽取。它不对一手调查数据执行子样本稳健性检验，§4.2 报告的 Driver/Both 与 Rider-only 拆分等分析需要人工介入。它也不会将稿件中的设计主张与已实现代码库或已部署数据结构交叉核对——§5.8.3 的机制更正是通过人工检查 rating.controller.js、ratings 表 DDL（数据定义语言，Data Definition Language，即建表语句）与线上 point_rules 目录完成的，并记录为上文第三个负向审计实例。我们建议未来的使用者将其视为人类判断的结构化与审计辅助，而非替代品，并建议设计案例类论文为其配套一道明确的「实现与稿件对账」工序。

7Discussion讨论

7.1General Reflections总体反思

From grassroots to formal. .edu verification occupies a middle ground between commercial rideshare platforms (where identity is verified but community is absent) and informal WeChat groups (where community exists but identity is unverified). CampusRide positions institutional identity as a third trust architecture—one that is scoped by shared affiliation rather than by market participation or social proximity.

从草根到正式。.edu 验证占据了商业网约车平台（身份经验证但社区缺席）与非正式微信群（社区存在但身份未验证）之间的中间地带。CampusRide 将机构身份定位为第三种信任架构——其范围由共同隶属关系划定，而非由市场参与或社交亲近度划定。

Cross-module transferability. The four design primitives are not carpool-specific. The identity layer is shared across all six modules; the messaging embedding creates cross-module coordination channels; and the points meta-layer links incentives across modules. This suggests a methodological claim: design primitives distilled from one module's deep-dive can propagate to other modules within the same identity-scoped platform, though only the carpool module received deep-dive treatment.

跨模块可迁移性。四个设计基元并非拼车专属。身份层为全部六个模块共享；消息嵌入创造了跨模块协调渠道；积分元层将激励跨模块串联。这提示了一个方法学主张：从一个模块的深入剖析中提炼的设计基元，可以在同一身份限定平台内传播到其他模块——尽管只有拼车模块得到了深入剖析。

Pipeline-assisted design research. The research-agent pipeline made the literature synthesis auditable and pushed adversarial scoping deeper than it would have been otherwise: the contradiction reviewer identified five focus areas that directly structured §7.2. At the same time, the pipeline's limitations—niche-category misclassification, absence of primary-data sensitivity analysis, and no implementation-versus-manuscript reconciliation—required manual correction, reinforcing that the pipeline is a complement to, not a substitute for, human judgment.

流水线辅助的设计研究。research-agent 流水线使文献综合可审计，并把对抗性界定推得比原本更深：矛盾评审识别出的五个焦点领域直接构成了 §7.2 的结构。与此同时，流水线的局限——小众类别误分类、缺少一手数据敏感性分析、没有实现与稿件的对账——都需要人工纠正，再次印证流水线是人类判断的补充而非替代。

What the soft launch taught us. The ~4-month soft launch on www.campusgo.college (§5.9) reshaped the manuscript in three ways that a design-without-deployment version could not have. First, inspecting the live ratings, rides, and point_rules tables against §5.8 surfaced the rating-mechanism overclaim corrected in §5.8.3: the implemented system enforces a 2-hour post-departure readiness delay, role-paired submission, and update-in-place revision, with neither a formal dispute window nor trend protection. Second, querying the Railway-backed production project exposed a deployment-versus-repo gap: the points subsystem (§5.8.4), fully specified in backend code, has zero point_rules rows and no point_transactions table in production, and all 184 user rows have users.points = 0. The gamification primitive is therefore a code-level affordance, not yet a behavioral lever. Third, the asymmetry between identity registration (184 verified @cornell.edu accounts) and module engagement (zero currently-live rides, activities, or ratings; 15 removed marketplace items; 5 single-member community groups) sharpens the cross-module framing: even a fully functional .edu gate does not spontaneously produce content on the other side. The WeChat notice bridge (82 pushes: 62 marketplace, 16 rideshare, 4 activity) is where the platform reaches back into the grassroots channels that the formative survey named, and is the surface where historical ride bookings left their most legible traces. These are formative observations, not deployment validations.

软启动教给我们的。在 www.campusgo.college 上约 4 个月的软启动（§5.9）以三种「无部署的纯设计版本」做不到的方式重塑了本稿。第一，将线上 ratings、rides 与 point_rules 表与 §5.8 对照检查，暴露出 §5.8.3 中已更正的评分机制夸大表述：已实现的系统强制执行出发后 2 小时的就绪延迟、角色配对提交与原地更新修订，既无正式争议窗口也无趋势保护。第二，查询 Railway 托管的生产项目暴露出部署与代码仓库之间的落差：在后端代码中完整定义的积分子系统（§5.8.4），在生产环境中 point_rules 为零行、point_transactions 表不存在，且全部 184 条用户记录 users.points = 0。因此游戏化基元只是代码层面的能力，尚非行为杠杆。第三，身份注册（184 个已验证 @cornell.edu 账号）与模块参与（零条当前有效的行程、活动或评分；15 件已下架的二手商品；5 个单人社区群组）之间的不对称，使跨模块的论述框架更加锐利：即便一个完全运转的 .edu 门槛，也不会在门内自发地产生内容。微信通知桥（82 条推送：62 二手市场、16 拼车、4 活动）是平台反向触达形成性调查所点名的草根渠道之处，也是历史行程预订留下最清晰痕迹的界面。这些都是形成性观察而非部署验证。

7.2Adversarial Scoping and Limitations对抗性界定与局限

Formalization risk. Formalizing grassroots coordination practices into a platform may reproduce the opacity, autonomy loss, and asymmetrical oversight associated with algorithmic management [Keegan2025; Kinowska2022; Weber2023]. Our rating-fairness design (§5.8.3) is a response to this risk, not a solution; whether it prevents the emergence of platform-mediated harm remains untested.

形式化风险。将草根协调实践形式化为平台，可能复制算法管理所伴随的不透明、自主性丧失与不对称监督 [Keegan2025; Kinowska2022; Weber2023]。我们的评分公平性设计（§5.8.3）是对这一风险的「回应」，而非「解决方案」；它能否阻止平台中介伤害的出现，仍未经检验。

Three-layer sample skew. Our findings are scope-limited by three layers of sample skew: (a) Language skew: 72 of 91 respondents reporting native language (79%) were Mandarin-native; only 15 (16%) were English-native. Any claim about English-speaking students—including that they do not use WeChat groups—carries substantial uncertainty. (b) Driver-subset skew: The F5 rating-fairness asymmetry rests on the Driver/Both subset (N=19), which is methodologically preferred over the full-sample mix but still small and requires a dedicated driver-side survey to confirm. The Rider-only control (N=11–12) is reported for transparency, not because its weaker pattern invalidates the finding. (c) Completion skew: Only 44 of 111 eligible respondents (40%) completed the full survey; finishers may differ systematically from non-finishers in carpool salience or engagement.

三层样本偏斜。我们的发现受三层样本偏斜的范围限制：(a) 语言偏斜：报告母语的 91 名受访者中 72 名（79%）为普通话母语，仅 15 名（16%）为英语母语。任何关于英语学生的主张——包括「他们不使用微信群」——都带有相当大的不确定性。(b) 司机子样本偏斜：F5 评分公平性不对称依托于 Driver/Both 子样本（N=19），方法上优于全样本混合，但样本仍小，需要一项专门的司机侧调查予以确认。Rider-only 对照（N=11–12）的报告是出于透明，并非因为其较弱模式会推翻该发现。(c) 完成度偏斜：111 名合格受访者中仅 44 名（40%）完成全部问卷；完成者在拼车关注度或参与度上可能与未完成者存在系统性差异。

No deployment evaluation. This paper stops at the design-case stage. The four carpool design decisions have not been compared in a deployed setting, and any claim about their relative effectiveness remains design-motivated rather than usage-validated [Tushev2022; Fradkin2021].

无部署评估。本文止步于设计案例阶段。四项拼车设计决策未在已部署环境中进行比较，任何关于其相对有效性的主张都仍是设计驱动而非使用验证的 [Tushev2022; Fradkin2021]。

Scope boundary of .edu. .edu verification confirms institutional affiliation; it does not verify driving competence, safe conduct, legal accountability, or governance quality [Mufumbiro2026]. Identity verification addresses identity problems but not behavior problems, and it does not substitute for platform governance.

.edu 的范围边界。.edu 验证确认机构隶属关系；它不验证驾驶能力、安全行为、法律可问责性或治理质量 [Mufumbiro2026]。身份验证解决身份问题而非行为问题，亦不能替代平台治理。

Gamification risk. Cross-module points may invite gaming rather than trustworthy participation: points can become targets in themselves, creating incentives for fake trips, strategic compliance, or pressure-inducing behavior [Cheon2025; Zhang2025algo]. Work-games and algorithmic-control studies make such gaming plausible [Wu2026algo], but we do not claim measured abuse in CampusRide itself.

游戏化风险。跨模块积分可能招致刷分而非可信参与：积分本身可能成为目标，催生虚假行程、策略性应付或施压性行为的动机 [Cheon2025; Zhang2025algo]。劳动游戏（work-games）与算法控制研究使此类刷分具有现实可能性 [Wu2026algo]，但我们并不声称在 CampusRide 自身中观测到了已被度量的滥用。

8Conclusion结论

Small-town campuses present a multi-faceted coordination gap that extends beyond transportation; international students' grassroots practices give this gap a concrete shape. CampusRide formalizes these practices into a six-module identity-verified platform, sharing four design primitives across carpool, marketplace, activities, groups, messaging, and points modules. The five design decisions in the carpool deep-dive are specified against the deployed controllers, services, and schemas that back them, and are situated against a ~3.5-month soft launch on www.campusgo.college (184 verified Cornell users; zero currently-live rides, ratings, or points transactions) whose 2026-04-23 snapshot we report as formative implementation signal rather than as evaluation. The Driver/Both-subset rating-fairness observation (F5) remains the paper's most counterintuitive contribution: occasional campus drivers are least tolerant of rating-system unfairness, not of passenger behavioral misbehavior. The research-agent pipeline, contributed as an open-source methodology tool, provides an auditable evidence chain for design-research literature synthesis, including systematic contradiction surfacing for honest scoping, and its limits—including the absence of an implementation-versus-manuscript reconciliation—are reported alongside its use.

小城镇校园呈现出一个超越交通范畴的多面向协调缺口；国际学生的草根实践赋予了这一缺口具体形态。CampusRide 将这些实践形式化为一个六模块、身份验证的平台，在拼车、二手市场、活动、群组、消息与积分模块间共享四个设计基元。拼车深入剖析中的五项设计决策均对照支撑它们的已部署控制器、服务与数据结构加以表述，并置于 www.campusgo.college 上约 3.5 个月的软启动背景之下（184 名已验证 Cornell 用户；零条当前有效的行程、评分或积分流水），其 2026-04-23 快照作为形成性实现信号而非评估予以报告。Driver/Both 子样本的评分公平性观察（F5）仍是本文最反直觉的贡献：偶发性校园司机最不能容忍的是评分系统的不公，而非乘客的行为失当。作为开源方法学工具贡献的 research-agent 流水线，为设计研究的文献综合提供了可审计的证据链，包括服务于诚实界定的系统性矛盾揭示，而其局限——包括缺少实现与稿件的对账环节——也与其用途一并如实报告。
